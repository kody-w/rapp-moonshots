# Eight-team synthesis

## Majority architecture

The winning common architecture is sequential multimodal fusion:

```text
voice intent
  → radial options
  → coarse webcam gaze highlight
  → voice or gesture confirmation
  → reversible local action
```

The center orb never activates anything. Sensor loss freezes state and cancels
armed input.

## Final variants

| Track | Distinct hypothesis |
|---:|---|
| 01 Voice Orbit | Predictive radial intent can make voice refinement spatial and fast. |
| 02 Gaze Compass | Four-to-eight stable directions can make coarse webcam gaze reliable enough to be useful. |
| 03 Gesture Tunnel | Spatial depth and motion can make complex sequences memorable without menus. |

## Rejected claims

- pixel-precise eye tracking from any webcam,
- zero calibration,
- gaze as intent,
- irreversible gaze-only activation,
- always-listening offline speech in every browser,
- medical or universal accessibility claims, and
- OS-wide control from an ordinary browser tab.
