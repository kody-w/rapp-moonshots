# Pitch — depth you can remember

**Gesture Tunnel** replaces a brittle sequence of menus with a place.

The safe orb never moves. Six large tunnel mouths orbit it. Name intent with
voice, glance or move coarsely to preview, sweep to rotate, dip across a
threshold, and say **“choose”** to make it real. Each decision remains behind
you as a nested shell, so an eight-step route is visible history rather than
hidden application state.

The spectacle has a hard safety core: webcam estimates never commit, confidence
and neutral gates reject noise, camera loss freezes everything, and stop,
cancel, undo, recovery, and switch access are first-class. Frames are reduced
locally to motion centroids and discarded. The interface claims neither eye nor
hand tracking.

The deterministic run makes the argument in under four wall-clock seconds:
route three cobalt beacons, intentionally choose the wrong time, undo, lose the
camera, prove the freeze, recover, reach ORION-7 through North Gate, confirm,
and return home. Then inspect the exact replay and metrics JSON.

It is not a skin over forms. It is a reversible spatial memory for voice.
