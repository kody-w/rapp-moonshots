# Shark Tank pitch

## Hook — 10 seconds

Name the pain and show the surprising interaction.

## Customer

Who urgently needs this application?

## Problem

What expensive, frightening, slow, or impossible experience exists today?

## Invention

What new mechanism changes the experience?

## Live proof

Run the application and show the measured result.

## Why now

Which RAPP assets, agents, or fleet capabilities make this newly possible?

## Why moonshot

Explain the category-scale future without claiming the prototype already
achieves it.

## Business wedge

What narrow first use can create adoption?

## Risks

Name the strongest adversarial objection and current boundary.

## Ask

What should happen next if the evidence passes?
